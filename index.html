About me

I am a web application developer at UC Davis who enjoys building mobile apps in my spare time
I noticed that the app market is short of educational Arabic teaching apps for children, so I am trying to fill this gap and do my part in utilizing new technology to help benefit the children with something that will boost their knowledge and entertain them at the same time.
